      • Welcome to the world of coffee

      Coffee is a beverage brewed from roasted, ground coffee beans.

      Dark coloured and slightly acidic

      coffee has a stimulating effect on humans

      • It has the highest sales in the world market for hot drinks
      • Coffee production begins when the seeds of the Coffea plant's fruits (coffee cherries) are separated to produce unroasted green coffee beans.
      • The two most commonly grown coffee bean types are arabica and robusta.Coffee plants are cultivated in over 70 countries, primarily in the equatorial regions of the Americas, Southeast Asia, the Indian subcontinent, and Africa.
        • The beans are roasted and then ground into fine particles.
        • Coffee is brewed from the ground roasted beans, which are typically steeped in hot water before being filtered out.
        • It is usually served hot, although chilled or iced coffee is common.
